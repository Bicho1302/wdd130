Many people ask “What is sublimation printing and why choose to sublimate vs. screen print” or “What is sublimation? Sublimation is the process of transferring dye to a fabric using heat. The images and graphics are printed on special paper placed on the garment and heat is applied, allowing the ink to become part of the fabric. This will leave you with a more breathable, soft-hand feel to the fabric. The best part is that the numbers, letters, and graphics do not peel, wear off, or come out in the wash!

People interested in sublimation will visit this website to learn more about sublimation and the art you can create with this method. Surely different questions will be asked: How does sublimation work? What kind of things can you do with sublimation?
